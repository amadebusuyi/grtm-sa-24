About Summer Academy

One of the world's premier academic and research institutions, the Jonathan Carroll University has driven new ways of thinking since our 1876 founding. Today, JCU is an intellectual destination that draws inspired scholars to our Hyde Park and international campuses, keeping JCU at the nexus of ideas that challenge and change the world.

School of Entrepreneurship

Unleash your potential and ignite your entrepreneurial spirit. From ideation to execution, join us in mastering the art of business creation and innovation. Get ready to disrupt industries and shape the future of commerce.

Embrace Christian ethics as a guiding light in the workplace. Learn the principles of integrity, compassion, and servant leadership. Join us in cultivating a workplace culture rooted in faith, where ethics and excellence harmonize to inspire positive change.
